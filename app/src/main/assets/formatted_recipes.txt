Title: Berry Green Smoothie
Ingredients:
- 3 small bananas
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1–2 handfuls of spinach
- 1 cup frozen berries (blueberries, blackberries, etc.)
- 1/2 cup bran cereal such as All Bran original
- 1–2 tablespoons sweetener (sugar, honey, Truvia, agave, etc.)
- ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:
Blend bananas and milk until smooth. Add spinach and blend on a high setting until most of the spinach has been broken down into small pieces. Add the frozen berries and blend until the smoothie mixture is all one color.
Add the bran and sweetener; blend until desired consistency. Add ice cubes and blend again until smooth (optional – I usually don’t).

Title: Papaya Mango Smoothie
Ingredients:
- 2 large, ripe papayas
- 1 large, ripe mango
- 1 orange
- 3/4 cup coconut milk (I used the kind from a can)
- 1/2 cup regular milk
- 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:
Cut the papayas in half and remove the seeds. Cut the peel off the halves, roughly chop the peeled papaya and place in the blender. Cut a mango in half around the pit (there’s a large pit down the middle), scoop the flesh out of each half and place in the blender. Peel an orange, separate the sections, roughly chop into small pieces, and place in the blender.
Cover the fruit with the coconut milk, regular milk, and honey. Blend until very smooth. Add ice and blend until smooth, or serve chilled.

Title: Peach Green Smoothie
Ingredients:
- 2 cups frozen peaches
- 2 handfuls of spinach
- 1 fresh peach, pitted and chopped (optional)
- 1 cup water
- 1 tablespoon peeled, minced or grated fresh ginger
- 1 tablespoon honey + more to taste

Instructions:
Blend the frozen peaches, spinach, peach, water, and ginger until very smooth and homogenous in color. Add 1/4 cup water if needed to keep the mixture moving smoothly through the blender.
Add the honey, blend, and taste. Adjust as necessary (add more ginger, more honey, more water, etc). Serve immediately.

Title: Honey and Wild Blueberry Smoothie
Ingredients:
- 1 banana, fresh or frozen
- 1 cup mango chunks, fresh or frozen
- 1/2 cup wild blueberries, fresh or frozen
- 1/2 cup plain nonfat Greek yogurt
- 1/2 cup milk (or just enough to help things blend smoothly)
- 1 heaping tablespoon raw honey (more to taste)
- 1/2 cup kale or any other add ins that you want

Instructions:
Place bananas, mangoes, blueberries, yogurt, and milk in a blender. Blend until smooth. Add the kale and honey; blend again until smooth.
If desired (and especially if all your ingredients were fresh instead of frozen), add a few ice cubes to increase the volume of the smoothie and help it stay chilled. Crush until smooth.
Top with additional blueberries and serve immediately.

Title: Milk and Honey Iced Coffee
Ingredients:
- 1 Nespresso Altissio capsule
- 1 teaspoon honey
- 1/4 cup skim or almond milk
- 1/4 cup half and half
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
- 6–8 ice cubes

Instructions:
Brew the Altissio using a Nespresso VertuoLine System. It should make about 1/4 cup of espresso. Stir the honey into the hot espresso to dissolve. Add the milk, cream, and vanilla and stir to combine.

Title: Holiday Detox Green Apple Smoothie

Title: Classic Scrambled Eggs
Ingredients:
- 4 large eggs
- 1/4 cup whole milk
- 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Chopped chives (optional)

Instructions:
1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk together with the milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.
2. Heat the butter in a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat until melted and foamy.
3. Pour in the egg mixture and let it sit, without stirring, for a few seconds until it begins to set around the edges.
4. Using a spatula, gently stir the eggs, bringing the edges towards the center. Continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are just set but still slightly runny in places.
5. Remove from heat and let the residual heat finish cooking the eggs. Sprinkle with chopped chives, if using, and serve immediately.

Title: Spinach and Feta Omelette
Ingredients:
- 3 large eggs
- 1/4 cup fresh spinach, chopped
- 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, season with salt and pepper, and whisk until well combined.
2. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
3. Add the chopped spinach and sauté for 1-2 minutes until wilted.
4. Pour the eggs over the spinach, tilting the skillet to distribute them evenly.
5. Cook without stirring until the eggs begin to set, about 2-3 minutes.
6. Sprinkle the feta cheese over one half of the omelette.
7. Using a spatula, carefully fold the omelette in half over the cheese. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until the cheese is melted and the eggs are fully set.
8. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve immediately.

Title: Strawberry Shortcake
Ingredients:
- 1 quart strawberries, hulled and sliced
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
- 2/3 cup whole milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Whipped cream, for serving

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. In a medium bowl, combine the strawberries and 1/4 cup granulated sugar. Set aside to macerate.
3. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, 2 tablespoons sugar, baking powder, and salt.
4. Add the cubed butter and cut it into the flour mixture using a pastry cutter or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
5. Add the milk and vanilla extract, stirring just until the dough comes together.
6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, forming 8 shortcakes.
7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the shortcakes are golden brown.
8. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly.
9. To serve, split the shortcakes in half and layer with strawberries and whipped cream.

Title: Grilled Chicken Breast
Ingredients:
- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 8 hours.
3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
4. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the marinade.
5. Grill the chicken breasts for 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
6. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Title: Chicken Alfredo Pasta
Ingredients:
- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cups heavy cream
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 pound fettuccine pasta
- Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions:
1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
4. Reduce the heat to medium and add the heavy cream. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes.
5. Stir in the Parmesan cheese, ground nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens.
6. Add the cooked chicken and pasta to the skillet, tossing to coat with the sauce.
7. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley, if desired, and serve immediately.

Title: Chicken Caesar Salad
Ingredients:
- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 6 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
- 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 cup croutons

Instructions:
1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
2. Brush the chicken breasts with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
3. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
4. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
5. In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce, Caesar dressing, grated Parmesan cheese, and croutons.
6. Top the salad with the sliced grilled chicken and serve immediately.

Title: Classic French Toast
Ingredients:
- 4 slices of bread
- 2 large eggs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
- Maple syrup, for serving

Instructions:
1. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and ground cinnamon.
2. Heat the butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides.
4. Place the coated bread slices into the skillet and cook until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side.
5. Serve the French toast hot with maple syrup.

Title: Grilled Cheese Sandwich
Ingredients:
- 4 slices of bread
- 4 slices of cheddar cheese
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened

Instructions:
1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
3. Place two slices of bread, butter side down, in the skillet.
4. Top each slice with two slices of cheddar cheese.
5. Place the remaining slices of bread on top, butter side up.
6. Cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.
7. Remove from the skillet, slice in half, and serve hot.

Title: Bruschetta
Ingredients:
- 1 baguette, sliced into 1/2 inch slices
- 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
- 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Balsamic vinegar (optional)

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Arrange the baguette slices on a baking sheet and brush lightly with olive oil.
3. Bake the slices until they are golden brown, about 5-7 minutes.
4. In a bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, minced garlic, chopped basil, and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
5. Top each baguette slice with the tomato mixture.
6. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar, if desired, and serve immediately.

Title: Avocado Salad

- 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped

- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, combine the diced avocados, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro.

Title: Chicken Tacos
Ingredients:
- 1 lb chicken breast, diced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 packet taco seasoning
- 8 small tortillas
- 1 cup shredded lettuce
- 1 cup diced tomatoes
- 1/2 cup shredded cheese
- 1/4 cup sour cream
- 1 avocado, sliced
- Salsa (optional)

Instructions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add the diced chicken breast and cook until no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes.
3. Stir in the taco seasoning and 1/4 cup of water. Simmer until the sauce has thickened and the chicken is fully coated.
4. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
5. Assemble the tacos by placing the chicken in the center of each tortilla.
6. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream, avocado slices, and salsa if desired.
7. Serve immediately.

Title: Guacamole
Ingredients:
- 3 ripe avocados
- 1 lime, juiced
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup diced onion
- 3 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 roma tomatoes, diced
- 1 tsp minced garlic
- 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (optional)

Instructions:
1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt.
2. Mix in the diced onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic.
3. Stir in the cayenne pepper if using.
4. Refrigerate for 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.

Title: Beef Enchiladas
Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 packet enchilada seasoning mix
- 1 can (10 oz) enchilada sauce
- 8 flour tortillas
- 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- Chopped fresh cilantro (optional)

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef and chopped onion over medium heat until beef is browned and onion is tender.
3. Stir in the enchilada seasoning mix and 1/4 cup of water. Simmer until the sauce thickens.
4. Pour half of the enchilada sauce into the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
5. Spoon the beef mixture down the center of each tortilla. Roll up and place seam-side down in the baking dish.
6. Pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the top and sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese.
7. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
8. Serve with sour cream and chopped cilantro, if desired.

Title: Classic Milk Tea
Ingredients:
- 2 cups water
- 2 black tea bags (or 2 tablespoons loose black tea)
- 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
- 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
- Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:
1. Bring the water to a boil in a medium saucepan.
2. Remove from heat and add the tea bags. Let steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on desired strength.
3. Remove the tea bags and stir in the sugar until dissolved.
4. Add the milk and stir well.
5. If serving hot, pour the milk tea into cups and serve immediately.
6. If serving iced, let the tea cool to room temperature, then refrigerate until cold. Pour over ice cubes in glasses and serve.
